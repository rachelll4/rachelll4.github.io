Hello!

I'm an eager programmer who loves chewing on complex problems, but I'm also deeply interested in human stories. I dance along a spectrum from journalism to programming, sometimes as a full-time stringer and sometimes as purely a software developer. I've had the most fun in the middle: I've crafted deep-dive data stories, relying on R skills to bolster my conclusions, and I've built web components for huge news networks, taking requests from editors.

I love local news and empowering residents to take action and stay informed in their local communities. I also love to hike and am always looking for a new niche cafe!
